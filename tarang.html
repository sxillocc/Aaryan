About this program

Flying cars, Virtual computers, Increased Life expectancy, Automated machines everywhere, Personal Robot assistants, Solar Energy, Humans will go to Mars... this is what people thought 2020 would be like two decades back.

And we know where we are right now and what situation we are going through.

Our economic development plans are doomed, technology has failed and nature is telling us that we are not necessary here.

Today, when the entire world is engulfed in this Second Wave do we have a Second chance to make life and things better ?
