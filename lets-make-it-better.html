Let's Make it Better

Out of so many species in this universe, we, humans, stand a class apart due to our higher intelligence. In just one decade we have seen some of the most unimaginable technological advancements becoming reality. Despite all the latest innovations at our disposal, there is somewhere a pinprick in the society at large. Today the most dangerous disease is not HIV AIDS, TB, or COVID-19 but the way we are living our life. Mental anxiety, according to WHO, is the most widespread disorder, affecting more than 260 million people around the globe. We want to make life better by making things better. But statistics reveal that this approach has failed bitterly. "Let's Make It (LIFE) Better" the right way.

This 21 days workshop will definitely help you in making your life better and get connected to the roots of our glorious Vedic culture.
