About Us

The Aryans Club is the youth wing of Sri Sri Radha Rasabihari Temple, ISKCON Juhu. The club aims at educating and empowering today's youth on the foundation of the rich spiritual wisdom of Vedic India to overcome the obstacles of their progressive lives and make it a blissful journey in today's tough and competitive world.

Our Objective

1. Enhance the overall personality of an individual by improving traits like Public Speaking, Management & Leadership skills etc.

2. Strengthening mental health by improving concentration, self-confidence through correct life perspective.

3. Spreading awareness about the timeless wisdom given in our Vedic scriptures to know the origin of life and its purpose.

4. Teach the moral and ethical values from the lives of great personalities of ancient India.

5. Bringing people closer to Supreme Lord, Krishna, through the powerful mantra meditation and philosophical understanding of Bhagavad Gita.

6. To experience the divinity, arrange regular camps to the spiritual and natural places of Holy India.
