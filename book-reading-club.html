Books are the basis

ᐅ Group reading club | 15 mins daily | in your language | Online (in zoom application)

There are various sources to aquire knowledge and skillsets such as discourses, educational videos, internet, biographies, books, etc. But, do you know the root of all this sources?

The root of all this sources is Vedas. The word Veda is a sanskrit word which means knowledge. It contains vast pool of knowledge, may it be a astronomy, aeroplane science, medical science, all subject matters are covered in Vedas. And the essence or the ripen tastiest fruit of the tree of Vedas is Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam (Bhāgavata Purāṇa).

This initiative is to systematically study and understand the Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam. For that we are daily organising group reading meet where you can join with us and make your life purposeful.

Let's read together at your time, in your language.
