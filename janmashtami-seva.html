The Power of Giving


Krishna's blessings


Who can even forget the pastime of Baby Krishna along with fruit vendor, His hands full of grains, eagerly running towards the fruit vendor, bestowing blessings upon her? His palms were very small, and He was not very careful to hold the grains tight, so He was dropping them. The vendor saw this and was very much captivated and she immediately accepted whatever few grains were left in His palms and filled His hands with fruits. In the meantime, the vendor saw that her whole basket of fruit had become filled with jewels. The Lord is the bestower of all benedictions.

If someone gives something to the Lord, he is not the loser; he is the gainer by a million times.
